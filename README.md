# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior through a marketing funnel to understand how users move from website visits to final purchases. The goal is to identify drop-off points, measure conversion rates, and provide actionable recommendations to improve business performance.

This dashboard was created as part of **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Task 3 (2026)**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer conversion funnel.
- Measure conversion rates at each stage.
- Identify major drop-off points.
- Compare performance across traffic sources and visitor types.
- Generate business recommendations to improve conversions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Tableau Public/Desktop**
- **Microsoft Excel**
- **GitHub**

---

## 📂 Dataset
Dataset Used: **Online Shoppers Purchasing Intention Dataset**

Dataset Source:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/henrysue/online-shoppers-intention

The dataset contains:
- 12,330 website sessions
- 18 features related to user behavior and purchasing intention.

---

## 📊 Funnel Stages
1. Total Visitors
2. Engaged Visitors
3. High Intent Visitors
4. Customers

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Visitors
- Total Customers
- Overall Conversion Rate
- High Intent to Customer Conversion Rate
- Visitor to Engaged Conversion Rate
- Engaged to High Intent Conversion Rate

---

## 📌 Dashboard Components
### 1. Marketing Funnel Analysis
Visualizes customer movement through the funnel stages.

### 2. Conversion by Traffic Type
Identifies which traffic sources generate better conversions.

### 3. Conversion by Month
Shows monthly conversion trends.

### 4. Conversion by Visitor Type
Compares new and returning visitors.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Returning visitors have a higher conversion rate than new visitors.
- A significant drop-off occurs between the engagement and purchase stages.
- Certain traffic sources contribute more to conversions.
- Conversion rates vary across different months.

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Improve product pages to reduce customer drop-offs.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing traffic sources.
- Retarget engaged users who did not complete a purchase.
- Personalize offers for returning visitors.
- Optimize campaigns during high-conversion periods.
